# Contribution Guide

Thank you for contributing to AI Teacher Tools.

## How to Contribute

You can contribute by:
- Adding useful AI prompts
- Improving documentation
- Sharing educational workflows
- Fixing formatting or spelling issues
- Adding productivity resources

---

## File Structure

- lesson-prompts.md
- worksheet-prompts.md
- study-planner.md
- ai-classroom-workflows.md
- resources.md

---

## Contribution Rules

- Keep prompts clear and practical
- Use simple language
- Avoid copyrighted content
- Focus on educational usefulness

---

## Future Contribution Areas

- AI exam systems
- Smart grading workflows
- AI-powered attendance systems
- Interactive classroom tools
- AI research assistants

---

## Goal

Build a free open-source educational AI resource hub for teachers and students.
